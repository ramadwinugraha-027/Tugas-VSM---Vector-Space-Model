Artificial intelligence is widely used in the healthcare industry to improve medical services and patient care. Hospitals and research centers are developing AI systems that can analyze medical images, detect diseases earlier, and assist doctors in making treatment decisions. AI technologies are also used to process large amounts of patient data more efficiently and support medical research activities.

Many healthcare experts believe that artificial intelligence can increase the accuracy of diagnoses and reduce medical errors. During recent years, several hospitals have implemented AI-based systems to monitor patients and predict potential health risks. Although AI cannot fully replace doctors, researchers argue that it can become an important tool for supporting healthcare professionals and improving the quality of medical services in the future.
